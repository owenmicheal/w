Three (3) Days Chimpanzee and Bigodi Uganda Safari

This trip to Kibale National Park will make you know that spending one hour with chimps is exciting, but wait un til you spend a whole day with these Chimps while on a 3 Day Chimp Habituation. Kibale National Park is known as the primate capital because of the huge number of chimpanzees and other primate species that live in the park.
The 3 Day Chimp Habituation and or chimpanzee tracking safari is a great alternative to the 3-day goril la trekking safari especially considering the cost.

Trip Summary

  • Day 1: Arrival at Kibale National Park
  • Day 2: Chimpanzee Trek and Bigodi wetlands
  • Day 3: Chimpanzee Habituation
  • Day 4: Departure


    • Day 1:

    With hopes that your flight will be scheduled for an early morning landing in Uganda, our tour driver shall pick you from Kampala or Entebbe and depart for Kibale National Park. It is a 5 – 6 hours journey. You will stop over in Fort Portal town for your lunch. Thereafter you shall continue for a craters lake tour en-route to Kibale Nat ional Park. Enjoy the breathtaking landscape of rolling hills and crater lakes. You will take a hike to a great view point overlooking crater lakes.
    You will arrive at your lodge for dinner and overnight.

    Accommodation options:

  • Budget – Chimpanzee Forest Guesthouse, Kibale Forest Camp
  • Midrange – Turaco Treetops
  • Luxury – Papaya Lake Lodge, Primates Lodge Kibale, Kyaninga Lodge

    • Day 2: Chimpanzee tracking and Bigodi wetlands walk

    Morning: You shall have early breakfast and transfer to the park office by 7am. You will meet the park guides for quick briefing and set out for the chimpanzee tracking adventure in Kibale national park. You will be tracki ng one of the several chimpanzee groups in the Kanyanchu chimpanzee community that is full habituated. It may ta ke 1 hour or 2 to find the chimpanzees which can be heard screaming from a distance. You will spend a maximum of 1 hour with the chimps and then return to the lodge for lunch.

    Afternoon: After lunch you will go for a walk in the Bigodi wetlands. The Bigodi wetlands are situated on the ed ge of Kibale National Park. The wetlands harbor up to 8 species of primates and several other wildlife. After a wonderful nature experience in Bigodi wetlands you shall continue and visit the local communities near the wetl ands and learn about their cultures and traditions. You will then return to your lodge for dinner and overnight.

    Day 3: Chimpanzee Habituation:

    Morning: As early at 5am, you can do the chimpanzee habituation experience which allows up to 4 hours with the chimpanzees in a chimpanzee community that is still undergoing habituation.
    You will set out at 5am to find the chimpanzees before they are out of their nests, watch them as from the time they wake up and follow them for the rest of the day till afternoon when they are lazing around and relaxing a fter a busy morning. This experience gives time to get into the lives of the chimpanzees and see their differe nt ways throughout the day. It is also good for photography as you have time with the chimps.
    You may also enjoy experiential chimpanzee tracking by joining a small group of tourists to track the Ngogo chimpanzees community which is habituated and spend 1 – 2 hours. The experiential chimpanzee tracking does not have crowds which you may find in the ordinary chimpanzee tracking where several small groups of tourists track different groups of chimps in the same habituated chimpanzee community and can sometimes crisscross ea ch other during tracking.

    Day 4: Departure

    After your breakfast you shall drive back to Kampala or Entebbe. You will make a stopover at the Equator for a quick meal then proceed to Kampala. This will mark the end of the tour.
