Six (6) Days of Gorilla, chimpanzee and Wildlife Safari

This is an adventurous Uganda gorilla and wildlife safari package taking you on a memorable safari experience to 3 of Uganda’s finest safari destinations that include: Bwindi impenetrable national park, Queen Elizabeth national park and Lake Mburo National park.

Trip Summary

  • Day 1: Arrival at Queen Elizabeth National Park
  • Day 2: Morning Game drive and afternoon boat cruise on Kazinga Channel
  • Day 3: Drive to Bwindi impenetrable national park
  • Day 4: Mountain gorilla trekking at Bwindi NP
  • Day 5: Drive to Lake Mburo national park, game drives
  • Day 6: Nature walks and departure


    • Day 1: Arrival Day at Queen Elizabeth national park

    Morning: The six-day tour will begin after breakfast at your hotel of origin. Our driver will arrive at 7:00 a. m. to pick you up. Then, embark on a 5- to 6-hour journey to Queen Elizabeth National Park via Masaka, Mbarara, and other towns.
    There will be several stops along the way, including at the Uganda Equator, where you can stand in both the nort hern and southern hemispheres and experiment with how water swirls in different directions when placed in the no rthern and southern hemispheres, take photos, and even buy some of the beautiful souvenirs. Continue your journe y after your meals, and as you approach Bushenyi, you will be welcomed by the coolest air you have ever experien ced in Uganda, as well as lush tea plantations on the hilly slopes of Igara and Ishaka, through the Kalinzu Fore st Reserve until you reach Queen Elizabeth National Park. You'll go straight to your lodge and unwind.

    Accommodation option:

  • Budget: Mweya Peninsula, Ishasha Sector, and Kyambura Game Reserve.
  • Midrange: Bush lodge, Engiri safari lodge in Rubirizi, King fisher in Kichwamba, Ihamba Safari, Buffalo lodge
  • Luxury: Mweya safari lodge, Mazike safari lodge, Ishasha wilderness camp, Queen Elizabeth Katara lodge among others

    • Day 2: Game drive and afternoon boat cruise on Kazinga Channel

    Morning: After an early breakfast, we'll go on a game drive across the Kasenyi Plains, where we can hopefully see at least 200 different bird species as well as elephants, buffaloes, bushbucks, Defassa waterbucks, wartho gs, Uganda kobs, and tree climbing lions from Africa. Finally, return to the Lodge for lunch and some downtime as you get ready for the boat ride following this incredible 2- to 3-hour excursion.

    Afternoon: You'll take the well-known Kazinga Channel launch cruise, where you can see animals includ ing hippos, nile crocodiles, elephants, buffaloes, and Uganda kobs as well as bird species like kingfishers an d shoebill storks. You will head back to the Lodge for dinner and relaxation as you prepare for the next day.

    Day 3: Transfer to Bwindi Impenetrable National Park

    Morning: Breakfast will be served at leisure before departing for Bwindi Impenetrable National Park, "Uganda's Mountain Gorilla Capital," via the spectacular savannah grasslands of Queen Elizabeth National Park. You will pass through Ishasha sector, where you will see tree climbing lions, buffaloes, elephants, and Uganda Kobs. T hen stop for lunch at the Savannah Resort Hotel (Kihihi) before arriving at the park in the evening and check ing in.

    Accommodation option:

  • Budget: Broadbill camp, Trekkers Tervan Cottages, Rushaga Haven Lodges, Bwindi Gorilla Conservation Camp
  • Mid-range: Gorilla Mist Camp, Lake Kitandara Camp, Gorilla Resort, Gorilla Forest Camp, Buhoma homestead , Bwindi Volcanoes Lodge, Engangi Camp Uganda among others
  • Luxury: Nkuringo Bwindi Gorilla Lodge, Clouds Mountain Gorilla Lodge, Buhoma Lodge, Volcanoes Bwindi Lodg e, Chameleon Hill lodge among others

    • Day 4: Mountain gorilla trekking at Bwindi

    Morning: Given the physical demands of gorilla trekking, you will need a heavy breakfast before proceeding to the Park Headquarters for a pre-tracking briefing at 7:00 am. You will continue to the jungles at 8:00 am to tr ack the adorable mountain gorillas in their natural habitat characterized by thick forest covered by mist. Trek king lasts 2-6 hours and once sighted, you will be required to spend only one hour with the allocated gorilla f amily while studying their behavior and taking photos then proceed to the Lodge to relax.
    After a rewarding trek through the jungle, we sit in the forest among the mountain gorillas, listening to them c ommunicating with one another. Marvel at the sheer size of the dominant adult male gorillas, called Silverbacks and experience one of Africa’s most poignant wildlife encounters. After lunch in the forest we take a well-des erved rest or enjoy some bird watching.
    We return to the peaceful accommodation to relax and recuperate after our unforgettable gorilla trek in the Bw indi Forest of Uganda.

    Afternoon: In case you are still energetic, you will surely continue for a cult ural walk to Batwa villages to be immersed in their ancient lifestyles and be entertained in their traditional dances. We then return to our accommodation.

    Day 5: Transfer to Lake Mburo and afternoon launch trip

    Morning: After your breakfast, you will transfer to Lake Mburo National Park.
    While at Igongo cultural Centre, you will have a lunch stopover in the land of milk and honey. Here we lea rn about the Bahima and Ankole cultures of Uganda, gaining insight into the history of these interesting people.
    After lunch you will shortly embark on your journey to the Park.
    Afternoon: You will head out for the sunset launch trip to spot the hippos, Nile crocodiles as well as aquatic bird species. Take as many photos to make your adventure specifically remarkable.

    Accommodation option:

  • Budget: Rwonyo rest camp, The Eagles nest, Rangeland Mburo lodge
  • Mid-range: Rwakobo rock, Acardia cottages Mburo, Mpogo safari lodge
  • Luxury: Muhingo lodge, Mantana tented camp, Mburo safari lodge

    • Day 6: Nature walks and departure

    Walking through nature is a nice activity in the Lake Mburo national park. All guests who want to take guided nature walks through the park are welcome to do so; these tours are often led by a ranger guide. The guided hik es take you to the salt lick, which is likely the sweet spot for many animals, from Rwonyo, the park's adminis trative hub. Wildlife/animals parade themselves to the salt licks to savor the flavor of the salty licks crea ted of water and soil. The western shore of Lake Mburo is the location of a second nature walk that begins at 7:00 am and lasts for two hours. This tour ensures that you will see hippos emerging from the water and hyena s returning to their dens. A further fascinating walk takes you into the forest, revealing views of the birds that it supports. Different exciting treks take you through woodlands, providing chances to see other mamma ls, while hikes up a hill give you a perspective over the park's 9 sections rounded off with its 14 lakes . After the walk, you will return to the lodge, freshen up and checkout then Depart for Kampala or Entebbe. This will mark the end of your 6 day tour.
