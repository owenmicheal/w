Seven (7) Days Chimp, Wildlife and Gorilla Trekking Safari

You will enjoy travelling to 4 destinations that include; Kibale national park, Queen Elizabeth national park, Bwindi national park and Lake Mburo national park. These 4 places will offer you un forgettable wildlife expe rience that can be compared to none.

Trip Summary

  • Day 1: Arrival at Kibale National Park
  • Day 2: Chimpanzee tracking in the forests of Kibale National Park and drive to Queen Elizabeth National Park
  • Day 3: Morning Game drive and afternoon boat cruise on Kazinga Channel
  • Day 3: Transfer to Bwindi Impenetrable National Park
  • Day 3: Gorilla trekking and cultural walk in Bwindi Impenetrable Forest Park.
  • Day 3: Transfer to Lake Mburo and afternoon launch trip
  • Day 7: Morning game drive and departure


    • Day 1: Arrival at Kibale National Park

    Our 7-day Uganda Safari departs from Kampala or Entebbe. With hopes that your flight will be scheduled for an ea rly morning landing in Uganda, our tour driver shall pick you from Kampala or Entebbe and depart for Kibale Natio nal Park. It is a 5 – 6 hours journey. You will stop over in Fort Portal town for your lunch. Thereafter you shal l continue for a craters lake tour en-route to Kibale National Park. We hope that you will enjoy the breathtaking landscape of rolling hills and crater lakes. You will take a hike to a great view point overlooking crater lakes . You will then arrive at your lodge for dinner and overnight.

    Accommodation option:

  • Budget: Chimpanzee Forest Guesthouse, Kibale Forest Camp
  • Midrange: Turaco Treetops
  • Luxury: Papaya Lake Lodge, Primates Lodge Kibale, Kyaninga Lodge

    • Day 2: Chimpanzee tracking and drive to Queen Elizabeth National Park

    Day two starts with an early breakfast, after which you will be transferred to the park headquarters at Kanyanch u Visitor Center at 7:00 am for a pre-tracking briefing before entering the jungles of Kibale Forest. The dense jungles of Kibale Park are considered the best place to view chimpanzees in Africa.
    After 2-5 hours of tracking, you will hear them hooting or see them feeding, playing and grooming, and that’s when your cameras start rolling. Besides the chimpanzees, expect to see several primate species including the red-tailed monkeys, L’Hoest’s monkeys, black and white colobus monkeys and olive baboons among others as well as birds, butterflies and tree species of interest.

    You will then return to the Lodge for lunch and shortly check-out before starting your journey to Queen Elizabe th National Park in the afternoon. Meanwhile you will be able to view the glamorous ranges of the mountains of the moon. Furthermore, pass through Kasese town and enjoy the smiles from the local residents of the town. On arrival at the park, you will check in as soon as you reach the lodge.

    Accommodation option:

  • Budget: Mweya Peninsula, Ishasha Sector, and Kyambura Game Reserve.
  • Midrange: Bush lodge, Engiri safari lodge in Rubirizi, King fisher in Kichwamba, Ihamba Safari, Buffalo lodge
  • Luxury: Mweya safari lodge, Mazike safari lodge, Ishasha wilderness camp, Queen Elizabeth Katara lodge among others

    • Day 3: Game drive and afternoon boat cruise on Kazinga Channel

    Morning: After an early morning breakfast, we head for a game drive through the Kasenyi Plains where you are like ly to spot the African tree climbing lions and the elusive leopards, Uganda kobs, Elephants, buffaloes, bushbucks , Defassa waterbucks and warthogs in addition to at least 200 bird species. Lastly, after this amazing 2-3 hours ’ activity, head back to the Lodge for lunch and relaxation as you prepare to go for the boat cruise

    In the afternoon, you will go for the famous launch trip on the Kazinga Channel where Hippos, Nile crocodile s, elephants, buffaloes and Uganda Kobs, as well as bird species like Kingfishers and shoebill storks, can be spotted.

    Day 4: Transfer to Bwindi Impenetrable National Park

    Morning: Your breakfast will be served at leisure then check out of the Lodge before heading to Bwindi Impenetrable National Park, “Uganda’s mountain gorillas’ capital” through the spectacular savannah grasslands of Queen Elizabet h National Park. You will pass through Ishasha sector to see the tree climbing lions as well as the buffaloes, ele phants and Uganda Kobs. Then have a lunch stop at Savannah Resort Hotel (Kihihi) and arrive at the park in the eve ning and check in.

    Accommodation option:

  • Budget: Bwindi Gorilla Conservation Camp
  • Midrange: Lake Kitandara Camp, Gorilla Resort , Gorilla Forest Camp, Buhoma homestead, Bwindi Volcanoes Lodge, Engangi Camp Uganda among others
  • Luxury: Nkuringo Bwindi Gorilla Lodge, Cloud s Mountain Gorilla Lodge, Buhoma Lodge, Volcanoes Bwindi Lodge, Sanctuary Gorilla Forest among others

    • Day 5: Gorilla trekking and cultural walk in Bwindi Impenetrable Forest.

    Morning: Given the physical demands of gorilla trekking, you will need a heavy breakfast before proceeding to the Park Headquarters for a pre-tracking briefing at 7:00 am. You will continue to the jungles at 8:00 am to t rack the adorable mountain gorillas in their natural habitat characterized by thick forest covered by mist. Trekking lasts 2-6 hours and once sighted, you will be required to spend only one hour with the allocated g orilla family while studying their behavior and taking photos then proceed to the Lodge to relax.
    After a rewarding trek through the jungle we sit in the forest among the mountain gorillas, listening to them communicating with one another. Marvel at the sheer size of the dominant adult male gorillas, called Silv erbacks and experience one of Africa’s most poignant wildlife encounters. After lunch in the forest we take a well-deserved rest or enjoy some bird watching.
    We return to the peaceful accommodation to relax and recuperate after our unforgettable gorilla trek in the Bwindi Forest of Uganda.

    Afternoon: In case you are still energetic, you will surely continue for a cultural walk to Batwa villages to be immersed in their ancient lifestyles and be entertained in their traditional dances. We then return to our accommodation.

    Day 6: Transfer to Lake Mburo and afternoon launch trip

    Morning: After your breakfast, you will transfer to Lake Mburo National Park.
    While at Igongo cultural Centre, you will have a lunch stopover in the land of milk and honey. Here we learn about the Bahima and Ankole cultures of Uganda, gaining insight into the history of these interesting people.
    After lunch you will shortly embark on your journey to the Park.
    Afternoon: You will head out for the sunset launch trip to spot the hippos, Nile crocodiles as well as aqua tic bird species. Take as many photos to make your adventure specifically remarkable.

    Accommodation option:

  • Budget: Rwonyo rest camp, The Eagles nest, Rangeland Mburo lodge
  • Mid-range: Rwakobo rock, Acardia cottages Mburo, Mpogo safari lodge
  • Luxury: Muhingo lodge, Mantana tented camp, Mburo safari lodge

    • Day 7: Morning game drive and departure

    In the early morning, you will have your breakfast and then embark for the game drive conducted through varying l andscapes (rocky outcrops, acacia woodland and hillsides among others) where you will get the chance to see a varie ty of wildlife species including the zebras, giraffes, impalas, Topis, elands, waterbucks, bushbucks in addition to the bird species that call the Park home. After the drive, you will return to the lodge, freshen up and checkout then Depart for Kampala or Entebbe.
